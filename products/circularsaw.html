Circular Saw

A circular saw is a power-saw using a toothed or abrasive disc or
blade to cut different materials using a rotary motion spinning
around an arbor. A hole saw and ring saw also use a rotary motion
but are different from a circular saw. Circular saws may also be
loosely used for the blade itself.
